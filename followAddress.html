PART 4: Follow The Address

With great enthusiasm, he wanted to follow the address. He jumped on his old jeep the next day early in the morning and drove into town. The address directed him to a peaceful neighbourhood with high covenent business houses. He knocked on the doorbell of the house in the address. To his surprisee it was his youngest son welcoming him with a hug and tears. He lived with his son till he died a HAPPY OLD MAN. THE END.
